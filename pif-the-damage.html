The DRIFT Token Crash

What happened to the token
Before the exploit, DRIFT traded at $0.069 on Solana DEXes. Normal day. A couple of swaps per hour. Then at 16:06 UTC the vaults drained. Within one hour, the token crashed to $0.026 — a 64% drop at the bottom. It partially recovered to $0.050 by evening, then continued sliding. As of April 3, DRIFT trades at $0.034. It has lost 51% of its pre-exploit value and hasn't recovered.

The Panic

13,280 swaps in one hour
Before the exploit, DRIFT saw 19-33 DEX swaps per hour. Normal trading. At 17:00 UTC — one hour after the drain — the volume exploded to 13,280 swaps. That's a 400x increase. 5.5 million DRIFT tokens changed hands in sixty minutes. Click any bar below for details.
